The MetaBrainz Foundation Projects

MusicBrainz

MusicBrainz is a community-maintained open source music encyclopedia that collects music metadata and makes it available to the public.

MusicBrainz Picard

MusicBrainz Picard is an open-source software application for identifying, tagging, and organizing digital audio recordings.

Cover Art Archive

Cover Art Archive is a database of cover art corresponding with the releases in MusicBrainz. Their goal is to make cover art images available to everyone on the Internet in an organized and convenient way.

CritiqueBrainz

CritiqueBrainz fills the gap between music critics and raw data by providing a platform created for the sole purpose of Creative Commons licensed music reviews.

AcousticBrainz

AcousticBrainz aims to crowd source acoustic information for all music in the world and make it available to the public. Their goal is to provide a massive database of information about music.

BookBrainz

BookBrainz is an online encyclopedia containing information about published literature. Their aim is to collect and store data about every book, magazine, journal, or other publication ever written.

ListenBrainz

ListenBrainz is an open source music website that allows users to import their listen history. They hope that they will be able to provide enough data for developers to create new music recommendation systems.

MessyBrainz

MessyBrainz identifies unclean or misspelled metadata, and where possible, links it to stable MusicBrainz identifiers. MessyBrainz is being used within AcousticBrainz and ListenBrainz.
